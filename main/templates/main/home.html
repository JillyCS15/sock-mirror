    SHACL on Completeness Knowledge
    Completeness is an essential quality aspect concerning the breadth, depth, and scope of knowledge. SoCK is a tool to provide & manage SHACL patterns and shapes for completeness about knowledge graphs. Try SoCK now!
    What is SoCK?
    SoCK is short for SHACL on Completeness Knowledge. SoCK specializes in dealing with completeness in knowledge graphs using patterns. Check out the video to learn more about SoCK!
